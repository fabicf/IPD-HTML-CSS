Homemade
honey butter

Ingredients

  • 16 oz mason jar
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Cold water
  • 1/2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Fill jar halfway with heavy cream, screw the lid on tight.
  2. Shake mason jar for 5-8 minutes.Ater the first 2-3 minutes, it'll be whipped cream. Keep shaking the jar until you hear that a lump has formed.
  3. Shake an additional minute, then remove solids from the jar.
  4. Place solids into small bowl, pour a splash of cold water over the butter until you can form it into one piece.
  5. Add honey and salt to butter; mix until fully combined. Chill in the refrigerator for an hour.
